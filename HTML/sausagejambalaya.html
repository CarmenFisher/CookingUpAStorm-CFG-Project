Sausage Jambalaya

Ingredients

  • 200g rice
  • 1tbsp olive oil
  • 8 pork sausages
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 x 400g tin chopped tomatoes
  • 250g frozen mixed veg

Method

  1. Put rice in a sieve and rinse thoroughly under cold water for about 1 minute.
  2. Heat the oil in a large pan and brown the sausages for a few minutes. Stir in the Cajun seasoning and onion, and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add the rice to the pan and cook for another 30 seconds, then pour in the chopped tomatoes and 400ml water.
  4. Bring to the boil, lower the heat, cover with a lid and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in the frozen veg and cook for another 10 minutes. Season. Serve piping hot.
