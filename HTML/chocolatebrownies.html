Chocolate Brownies

Ingredients

  • 350g dark chocolate, broken into small pieces
  • 250g unsalted butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 250g dark brown sugar
  • 100g plain flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Method

  1. Heat the oven to 170 C.
  2. Lightly grease a square cake tin.
  3. Melt the chocolate and butter in a heatproof bowl over a pan of simmering water.
  4. Whisk the eggs and sugar together for 2-3 minutes until smooth and fluffy.
  5. Fold the chocolate mixture into the egg mixture.
  6. Sieve the flour and the baking powder into the mixture and stir into the mix.
  7. Pour the mixture into the cake tin.
  8. Place on the middle shelf of the oven and cook for 35 minutes.
  9. Remove from the oven and allow to cool before serving.
