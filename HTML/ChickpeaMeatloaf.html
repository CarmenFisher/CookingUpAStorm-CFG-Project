Chickpea Vegan Meatloaf

Ingredients

  • 500g Chickpeas dried
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely diced
  • 2 carrots, finely grated
  • 40g sesame seeds
  • ½ tsp Coriander seeds
  • Breadcrumbs as needed
  • 1 onion, finely diced
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp, egg substitute, powdered
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 dried chilli

Method

  1. In a bowl, cover chickpeas with water and leave to soak overnight.
  2. Heat the oven to 180°C (160°C in a fan oven), 375°F, and brush the loaf tin with oil.
  3. Drain the chickpeas well and pass through a mincer.
  4. Fry the onion and garlic in 1 tbsp hot oil until translucent, then add to the chickpea mixture.
  5. Add the carrots, egg substitute and sesame seeds.
  6. Grind the cumin, coriander and chilli in a mortar and add to the chickpea mixture.
  7. Season the vegan meatloaf with salt and ground black pepper, add 1 tbsp oil and mix together thoroughly.
  8. If the mixture is too soft, add breadcrumbs.
  9. Check the seasoning then transfer to the tin, smooth the top flat and brush with the remaining oil.
  10. Bake in the oven for 1 hour.
  11. If it starts browning too much, cover the vegan meatloaf with aluminium foil.
