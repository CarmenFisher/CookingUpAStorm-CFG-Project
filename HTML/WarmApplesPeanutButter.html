Sausage and Penne Pasta

Ingredients

  • 2 Cox Apples, peeled, cored and cut into chunks
  • 1 tbsp of water
  • 1 tsp Cinnamon (optional but recommended)
  • 1 tbsp Peanut Butter or any nut butter and as much as desired!

Method

  1. Place apple chunks in a microwave proof bowl
  2. Pour in the water over the apples
  3. Place bowl int he microwave and heat for 2 mins
  4. Once the apples have started to go soft, take the bowl out of the microwave and mix in a tablespoon of the Peanut butter to the warm apples
