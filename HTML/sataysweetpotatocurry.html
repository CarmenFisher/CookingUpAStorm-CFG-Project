Satay Sweet Potato Curry

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp coconut boil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, grated
  • thumb-sized piece giner, grated
  • 3 tbsp Thai red curry paste
  • 1 tbsp smooth peanut butter
  • 500g sweet potato, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 400ml can coconut milk
  • 200g bag spinach
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • cooked rice, to serve (optional)
  • dry roasted peanuts, to serve (optional)

Method

  1. Melt the oil in a saucepan over a medium heat and soften the onion for 5 mins.
  2. Add the garlic and ginger, and cook for 1 min unti fragrant.
  3. Stir in the curry paste, sweet potato and then add the coconut milk and 200ml water.
  4. Bring to the boil, turn down the heat and simmer, uncovered, for 25-30 mins or until the sweet potato is soft.
  5. Stir though the spinach and lime juice and season well.
  6. Serve with cooked rice, if you like, and if you want some crunch, sprinkle over a few dry roasted peanuts.
